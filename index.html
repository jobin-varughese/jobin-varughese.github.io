about

I observe, build, and study systems that learn how people learn — using behavioral data, AI, and human-centered design to make education more responsive, equitable, and evidence-driven for today's learner.

research areas
AI in Education
Studying how AI shapes the learning process, from adaptive systems and intelligent feedback to LLM-as-Judge evaluation frameworks, and the challenge of keeping the human meaningfully in the loop.
Learning Analytics
The classroom generates more data than anyone knows what to do with. I work on the 'what to do with it' part — connecting behavioral signals and instructional design to surface patterns that help educators make better decisions.
Human-AI Interaction
What happens when AI enters spaces that used to belong entirely to humans? I am curious about trust, transparency, ethics, and privacy and the design of systems that feel like help rather than surveillance.
